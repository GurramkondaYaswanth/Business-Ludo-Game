Business Ludo Instructions

Brief about the Game

1. Two teams will be there with 3 pawns each, at there homes in the starting, you need to take all of ur 3 pawns to the end point first, before the coins with you gets over to win, you need 1 from dice roll to get the pawn into the Board to move at starting, and you wil be also getting only once chance to roll the dice, each time.

2. Coins will be reduced from ur account each time u step into the new box, the money reduced from u will be same as the mentioned in the box

3. While moving in the board u can kill the opposite pawn when u get to the same palce of the opposition pawn, except at the 1st box

4.In the game each team can use 6 times the magic card at any point of the game to win the surprises, each time u should pay 500 coins to flip the card

5.U can also make any box as your safe box, so that opposite pawn will not be able kill ur pawn at that box. For that u need to pay 5 times the box cost and ur opp team will pay u the amount on the box to u whenever they come to that box

About Magic card

1.Magic card contains surprises like wining coins from 0 to 2000 coins and also u can move ur pawn upto 7 places front without paying the coins and u can also get a chance to kill ur opposition pawn

2.You need to press the button team1 flip for team1 and team2 flip for team2 to win the surprises, the surprise details will be present on the card itself

3.Each time u flip 500 coins will be reduced from ur account

About safe box

1.For making any box as your safe box, so that opposite pawn will not be able kill ur pawn at that box u need to press the safe box button and choose the pawn position. u should choose ur pawn positions only

2.For that u need to pay 5 times the box cost and ur opp team will pay u the amount on the box to u, whenever they come to that box

About buttons

Roll dice

For rolling the dice and the value is displayed on below of the roll dice button

Roll dice button below the team1 is for team1 and roll dice button below the team2 is for team2

Pawn buttons

After roll dice, You need to select the pawn button corresponding to the desired pawn of ur teams to move. Pawn position will be displayed in the right of every pawn button

Team1/Team2 flip

If team1/Team2 want to use the magic card then You should click on this button

Safe box

If a team wants to make a box as safe box then they need to click this button and you can buy unlimited number of safe boxes until u can offord

FAQ's

After the magic card displays that you have a got a chance to kill the opponent pawn, then there will be display of 3 buttons on the top of the safe box butoon choose the button name of which pawn to be killed

After the magic card displays that you have a got a chance to move +5 or +7 position front, then there will be display of 3 buttons on the top of the safe box butoon choose the button name of which pawn to be moved


How many times can we use magic card and safe box in a match?

magic card: 6 per team

safe box: unlimited until u can offord

opponent pawn gets killed gets killed
